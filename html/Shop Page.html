Sub Gun Shingle

$45.00

The sub gun shingle is a elastic magazine and accessory holder that allows for a quick, simple, and streamline way to attach your 9mm magazines to cummerbund style slick carriers. The three middle cells include kydex inserts that are beveled at the ends to allow for easy reinsertion of the magazine even when fully collapsed. These inserts also allow for anything from the bulky cz scorpion magazines to the slim extended glock mags to be fully retained in the shingle. The 2 remaining cells do not have the the kydex inserts but are perfect for torniquetes, flashlights, multitools, or even two other magazines.

Compatible with most if not all sub gun magazine with the exception of the sig MPX. It has been tested with Cz scorpion, mp5, stribog, and extending Glock mags.

Made in the USA of berry compliant material these products are made to last.

Included:
1 9mm shingle with 3 kydex inserts
