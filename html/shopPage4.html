556 Shingle

The Infinity Tactical Solutions 556 Shingle is an elastic magazine and accessory holder designed to follow the Trex Arms 556 shingle. This slim design allows for quick and simple way to attach your 556 magazines to any cummerbund style plate carrier. This model does not include kydex inserts like with our sub gun shingle as we feel it is not necessary but it can be added. The 3 elastic middles cells fit the magazines securely, while the 2 remaining cells are perfect for accessories like tourniquets, flashlights, multitools or extended pistol magazines.

Ranger Green, Coyote Brown, Black

Made in the USA of berry compliant material these products are made to last.
